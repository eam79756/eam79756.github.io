Georgia Football

By: Emily Ann Martin

The Georgia football team has followed up last years National Title team with an identical 11-1 record during the regular season. The Bulldogs now go to Atlanta to face the #1 team in the country, the Alabama Crimson Tide. It will be a tough test for our Dawgs, but given the way they have played on both sides of the ball during the last month, many beleive this team has as good a chance as any to take down the vaunted Tide.

A good football team can only go as far as their quarterback can take them. Luckily for the dawgs, the have two stellar quarterbacks in Jake Fromm and Justin Fields. jake Fromm, as a true freshman, led UGA to an SEC Title and was one blown coverage away from being a National Champion. Justin Fields was the number one recruit coming out of high school in 2017 and reminds many SEC fans of Cam Newton, who happened to have a pretty good career.

Uga has a history of quality guarterbacks ranging from DJ Shockley to Matthew Stafford. However, these two quarterbacks could go down in Uga lore if they are able to bring a title home to Athens, which is a very real possibility. My all time favorite Georgia QBs are listed below:

  1. Aaron Murray
  2. Matthew Stafford
  3. Jake Fromm
